About Us

EZ Active was founded in 2019 by two enthusiastic Hong Kong football dads.

As parents frustrated with the available team management and notification tools at the time, they founded EZ Active to develop a useful, intuitive mobile app that everyone in a league could find useful.

What started as a small indie app project became the beginnings of EZ Active, a complete solution for sports team management and communications.

EZ Active focuses on custom solutions for sports league operators that need more specific features than can be found in a “one size fits all” team management app. We pride ourselves on developing the most useful user experience for each of our partnered leagues.
